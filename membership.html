Join the Golden Gate Tennis Club!

The Golden Gate Tennis Club is a Community Tennis Association based in San Francisco/Golden Gate Park for the purpose of fostering tennis participation in San Francisco.

Please note the annual membership is based on the calendar year. (Jan-Dec)

Benefits Include:

  • 3-4 GGTC Club Days per year- tennis events for club and non-club members
  • Tennis meet-ups
  • USTA Teams
  • Support of youth tennis and maintenance of courts
